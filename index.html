Play against a character

You are X and move first. Pick an opponent and start a game. Ace is provably unbeatable — the best you can do is a draw.

Tournament — the gauntlet

Play one game against every character in turn (you are X). Earn points (win 3, draw 1) and see if you can sweep the whole cast. Your run is saved — you can leave and come back.

Two-player

Pass the device back and forth. X goes first, then O, alternating.

Hot-seat is local practice — it does not affect your ranked match record in the Gym.

Play a friend with no server: make a move, copy the match code, and send it to your opponent (chat, email, anywhere). They paste it, move, and send a new code back.
